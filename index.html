Vision Statement

At Acts Of Kindness Home Healthcare, we envision a future where every individual receives compassionate and personalized care in the comfort of their own home.

We aspire to become recognized for empathy and excellence in the community we serve as well as the healthcare industry. Where our commitment to kindness, integrity and services that improve the delivery of care, to better our clients outcome.

Through our dedication to providing holistic, dignified and professional healthcare services, we aim to uplift communities and inspire a brighter, healthier tomorrow by spreading Acts of Kindness.

Mission statement

At Acts of Kindness Home Healthcare Agency our mission is to provide exceptional and compassionate home healthcare services utilizing our skills and tasks to meet our clients needs enabling them to live comfortably and independently in the comfort of their own home.

We are dedicated to delivering personalized care that promote dignity, respect, and well being for each individual we serve. Through our commitment to kindness, professionalism, and excellence,we strive to make a positive difference in the lives of our clients and their families every day. Encouraging others to spread Acts of Kindness!

Services

Light housekeeping, outings (doctor's appointment, grocery store), baths and dressing, prepare meals, companionship, medicine reminder, assistant with mobility and much more.
